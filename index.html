Welcome to HSL

...Nigeria's s first University-based open living lab for entrepreneurs, investors and startups.

We Ideate

Brainstorm and generate new business ideas that align with market needs, technological trends and the lab's expertise.

1

We Validate

Conducts market research, competitor analysis, and feasibility studies to assess the viability and potential success of each idea.

2

We Incubate

Startups receive guidance and resources to develop their products or services, build minimum viable products (MVPs), and refine their business strategies.

3

We Accelerate

Scale up startups, provision of intensive support like funding, strategic partnerships, etc. to help startups grow rapidly and achieve their business goals.

About HSL

Hebron Start-up Lab (HSL) is Nigeria's s first University-based open living lab for entrepreneurs, investors and startups. At HSL, we take ideas to concepts, make them products and finally send them to the market.

Our Communities

The HSL community is made up 4 arms - the creators, the creatives, the founders and the executors.

The Creatives

They are made up artistic, imaginative and innovative set of humans with minds for deep and great ideas and concepts. They include creative photographers, creative videographers, designers (artistic and UI/UX designers), etc.

The Creators

These individuals generate ideas and bring them to life. They visualize what they want to be and then find a means to bring their or others' ideas to life. They consist of web developers, programmers, and others.
